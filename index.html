About us

Kilkari Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to empowering underprivileged communities across India. Our mission is to provide essential support in areas such as food security, education, healthcare, and religious infrastructure. We aim to bridge the gap between compassionate donors and those in need, ensuring that every contribution leads to meaningful and lasting change.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Can I donate anonymously?

Yes, most platforms offer the option to donate anonymously. This means your name will not be displayed publicly on the list of donors. However, the campaign organizer will usually still have access to your name and email address for record-keeping purposes.

Is my payment information secure?

Reputable crowdfunding platforms use secure payment processing systems to protect your personal and financial information. Look for platforms that use SSL encryption and comply with PCI standards.

Are donations eligible for tax benefits?

Yes, all donations made to Satvik Daan Charitable Trust are eligible for 80G tax deductions as per Indian Income Tax laws.

Will I receive a receipt for my donation?

Yes, a donation receipt will be emailed to you immediately after your contribution, which you can use for tax purposes.

How will I know where my money is going?

We provide real-time updates and detailed impact reports for every initiative you support, so you can see how your donation is making a difference.

Can I choose which cause to support?

Yes, our platform allows you to pick the specific initiative—Food Security, Education, Healthcare, or Religious Infrastructure—that you wish to contribute to.

What is the minimum amount I can donate?

You can donate any amount you wish. Even ₹100 can make a real difference in someone’s life.

Can I make recurring donations?

Yes, we support recurring contributions. You can choose to give weekly, monthly, or quarterly as per your convenience.
